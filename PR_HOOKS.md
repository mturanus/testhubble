## ðŸ“° PR Hook Ideas

### **1. Industry Research & Reports**

**"State of Test Automation 2024"**
- Survey 500+ engineering teams
- Key findings:
  - Average time spent on test maintenance
  - Most common test automation challenges
  - ROI of test automation
  - Tool adoption rates
- Media angle: Data-driven insights
- Target publications: TechCrunch, HackerNoon, InfoQ

**"QA Budget Allocation Survey"**
- Survey engineering leaders
- Findings: How companies allocate QA budgets
- Media angle: Industry trends
- Target: B2B publications

**"Test Automation Tool Adoption Report"**
- Analyze tool usage trends
- Compare Playwright vs Cypress vs Selenium
- Media angle: Technology trends
- Target: Developer publications

---

### **2. Data-Driven Stories**

**"Companies Waste $X Million on Flaky Tests"**
- Calculate industry-wide cost
- Real data from customers
- Media angle: Cost of poor testing
- Target: Business publications

**"Test Automation Reduces QA Time by 70%"**
- Aggregate data from case studies
- Industry benchmarks
- Media angle: Efficiency gains
- Target: Tech and business publications

**"ROI of Managed Test Automation: $500K+ Annual Savings"**
- Real customer data
- ROI calculations
- Media angle: Business value
- Target: B2B publications

## ðŸ“§ Press Release Template

### **Structure**

**Headline:** [Hook] - [Key Benefit/Outcome]

**Subheadline:** [Supporting detail]

**Lead Paragraph:**
- Who, what, when, where, why
- Key hook/angle
- Main message

**Body:**
- Supporting details
- Quotes from founder/CEO
- Data/statistics
- Background information

**About Section:**
- Company description
- Contact information
